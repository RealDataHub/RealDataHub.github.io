Australia Animal Migration is a continuous research project exploring the migratory patterns of various species across the Australian continent.

Tracking animal movements provides vital insights into their behavior, ecological roles, and responses to environmental changes. This interactive application maps these journeys, highlighting the interconnectedness of habitats and the varying challenges animals face during regular seasonal migrations or due to human impact.

By interacting with visual analytics datasets, users can further explore spatial distributions to better understand the importance of conservation efforts across Australia's immensely diverse landscapes.
